    Data Visualization Projects

    Air Pollution Infographic

    This infographic highlights the significant drop in pollution levels across Malaysia during the MCO (Movement Control Order). It educates the public on six key pollutants (CO, NO₂, O₃, PM₁₀, PM₂.₅, SO₂), their health risks, and sources, while promoting sustainable practices to maintain lower pollution levels.

    Designed with simplicity and clarity in mind, this infographic ensures accessibility for all demographics. Using relatable examples and color-coded sections, it engages audiences and fosters awareness about air quality and environmental health.

    Data curated between 2018 and 2022 showcases the trends in pollutant concentrations, reinforcing the importance of maintaining sustainable habits post-MCO to protect high-risk groups in Malaysia.

    Tools Used

    • Adobe Illustrator
    • Canva

    References

    • Government of Malaysia. Monthly Air Pollution Data. data.gov.my

    Analyzing Income Disparities in Malaysia

    This project explores income inequality across Malaysia, shedding light on the disparities between different states and demographic groups. Using data visualization techniques, it provides insights into income trends and their implications for the economy and social well-being.

    The analysis emphasizes factors contributing to income inequality, including urbanization, education access, and economic opportunities. By presenting clear and actionable insights, this project encourages discussions around policy interventions and sustainable solutions to bridge income gaps in Malaysia.

    Tools Used

    • Tableau
    • Microsoft Excel

    Key Takeaways

    • Understanding income disparities in Malaysia.
    • Insights into potential policy interventions.

    References

    • UNDP Malaysia. Income Inequality Report. undp.org
